Bible Touch - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

HASEGI Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Bible Touch ("Fingertips of the Word") mobile application ("App").

Summary: Bible Touch is a Bible transcription app where users type Scripture by hand, verse by verse. We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality and do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information (via Social Sign-In):
    • Google Sign-In: Email address, profile picture (optionally provided)
    • Sign in with Apple: Email address (private relay email may be used)
    • Kakao Login: Email address, nickname, and profile picture (if consent is given)
  • Nickname: Display name set directly within the app for rankings and group participation.
  • Transcription Data: Bible verses typed and completed in Solo, Group, or Everyone modes.
  • Group Information: Group names, membership, and group-specific progress when participating in Group Mode.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and typing transcription statistics.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google AdMob collects your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android) for serving personalized ads.

1.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Service Infrastructure

We do not directly collect or store the following. However, third-party services we rely on may collect this information as part of their standard operations:

  • IP Address & Access Logs: Supabase (auth and database infrastructure), Google, Apple, and Kakao sign-in services may log your IP address for security purposes.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version may be collected by sign-in providers and Google AdMob.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted on secure cloud infrastructure to store your account and progress data.
  • Local Storage: Solo Mode data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.

3.2 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0 (Google, Apple, Kakao)
  • Row-level security policies for database access
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We May Share Your Information With:

  • Other Users: Your nickname and transcription statistics are visible on public leaderboards or within your groups.
  • Service Providers: Supabase (database hosting), Google (authentication, AdMob), Apple (authentication), Kakao (authentication).
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request.

4.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your email address publicly

5. Your Rights

  • View Your Data: Access your transcription progress and statistics within the app.
  • Update Information: Nickname editing will be available in a future app update.
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account directly from the in-app Settings menu. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately. This action cannot be undone as no backups are retained. For assistance: hasegi.cs@gmail.com
  • Advertising Preferences: On iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. On Android, go to Settings → Google → Ads. Premium purchase removes all advertisements entirely.

6. Children's Privacy

Bible Touch is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active.
  • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion and cannot be recovered.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In & AdMob: Google Privacy Policy
  • Sign in with Apple: Apple Privacy Policy
  • Kakao Login: Kakao Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Supabase Privacy Policy

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
