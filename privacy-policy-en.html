Bible Voice - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

HASEGI Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Bible Voice mobile application ("App").

Summary: Bible Voice is a Bible transcription app. We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality, including account information for social features (Group Mode, Everyone Mode) and transcription progress. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google or Apple Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture (if available).
  • Nickname: Display name you choose for rankings and group participation.
  • Transcription Data: Bible verses you complete in Solo, Group, or Everyone modes.
  • Group Information: Group names, membership, and group-specific progress when participating in Group Mode.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, transcription progress statistics.
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: Google and Apple provide basic profile information when you use their sign-in services.
  • Advertising Partners: Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and interaction data for personalized ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Account Management Email, name, profile picture
Transcription Progress Tracking Completed verses, chapter/book progress
Rankings & Leaderboards Nickname, transcription statistics
Group Features Group membership, group progress data
App Improvement Usage patterns, crash reports
Advertising Device identifiers, ad interaction data

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted on secure cloud infrastructure to store your account and progress data.
  • Local Storage: Solo Mode data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Data Location: Data may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate.

3.2 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure authentication via OAuth 2.0 (Google, Apple)
  • Row-level security policies for database access
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We May Share Your Information With:

  • Other Users: Your nickname and transcription statistics are visible on public leaderboards (Everyone Mode) or within your groups (Group Mode).
  • Service Providers:
    • Supabase (database hosting)
    • Google (authentication, advertising via AdMob)
    • Apple (authentication)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request.

4.2 We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your email address publicly
  • Use your transcription content for any purpose other than tracking progress

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: Access your transcription progress and statistics within the app.
  • Update Information: Change your nickname through app settings.
  • Delete Account: Request account deletion by contacting us at hasegi.project@gmail.com.

5.2 Advertising Preferences

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.
  • Premium subscription removes all advertisements.

5.3 Solo Mode Privacy

  • Solo Mode operates entirely offline with local storage only.
  • No account required; no data transmitted to servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Bible Voice is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active.
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Backups: May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
